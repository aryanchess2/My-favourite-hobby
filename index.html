Playing my guitar

About

Pluck the strings in order, thumb, index, ring, middle, and all over again. After the prelude, the rhythm follows, with a pick in hand, strum without restraints, just play out whatever tunes pop up in my mind. Guitar is my friend and it's always the best remedy. My hands are on it, my fingers shift naturally on different frets. In that very moment, listening to the harmony of six strings and humming the tunes in my mind.The happiness and music helps me regain my strength and reset myself back in order and carry on plucking thumb, index, ring, middle.

My favourite hobby

Playing the guitar is one of the most popular hobbies worldwide. Millions play the guitar as a leisure activity, as it is one of the easiest musical instruments to learn. Anybody can pick up the guitar and play some of their favorite songs in no time, if they just stick with it for a little while.It has a calming effect on the mind and body. Studies show that guitar not only reduces stress, but it helps to lower blood pressure levels as well.
